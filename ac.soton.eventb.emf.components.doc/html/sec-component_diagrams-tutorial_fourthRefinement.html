6.5 The Fourth Refinement

The washing machine sub-system is now further partitioned into 2 components: the drum sub-system and an abstract component representing the remaining washing machine sub-system, following the pattern of previous refinements. The component diagram is shown in Figure 40. Three boolean connectors pass messages from the washing machine sub-system to the drum to open and close the hot or cold water valves and to switch the drain pump on or off. Two further natural number connectors pass the water level and the water temperature back from the drum to the washing machine sub-system.
